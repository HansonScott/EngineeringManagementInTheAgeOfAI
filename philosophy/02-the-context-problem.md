# The Context Problem

---

> *This essay is in progress. It will cover: why EM work is fundamentally a context-maintenance problem, what it costs to rebuild context from scratch, and how AI's session amnesia makes this worse before it can make it better.*

**Core argument:** An Engineering Manager is, at root, a context holder. You hold context for your team members (their careers, their frustrations, their growth edges), for your team (its health, its direction, its gaps), and for your organization (what matters, what's shifting, what's expected). AI's session-to-session amnesia doesn't just create inconvenience — it makes the most context-dependent role in engineering harder to augment, unless you solve the persistence problem first.
